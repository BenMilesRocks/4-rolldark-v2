About Us

Rolldark: Your Premier Destination for DM for Hire and Professional Dungeon Master Services

At Rolldark, we lead a dedicated team of professional Game Masters specialising in Dungeons & Dragons and other roleplaying games across the UK.

Whether you're seeking a Dungeon Master for hire for your group of friends, family, or corporate event, we offer unparalleled gaming experiences tailored to your needs.

Why Choose Rolldark for DM for Hire Services?

Our mission is simple: to alleviate the burden of Dungeon Master responsibilities, allowing you and your group to immerse in the adventure fully. If you're searching for a DM for hire, Rolldark is your ultimate destination. We connect players with professional Paid DMs who excel in storytelling, acting, and creating captivating role-playing experiences.

Our Game Masters

All our game masters are highly skilled and come from a performance background, to ensure the most immersive table top roleplaying experiences for your games.

Robert Bradley

Rolldark Founder & Game Master | Oxford

Soon after this image was captured, Robert engaged in a fierce battle against a team of corporate Dungeons and Dragons enthusiasts. But little did they expect him to unleash the formidable Tiamat upon them. The players had this to say about their experience:

“As total beginners with Level 1 characters, we were expecting to face kobolds or perhaps even a goblin, but when the god of dragons appeared, our Level 1 characters lasted less than a millisecond.”

Robert, the author of the Reckoning Turbines Steampunk series, has been an avid Dungeons and Dragons player for the last 20 years. He says,

“Role-playing games have unlocked my creativity and have been a great tool for writers struggling with writer's block. Through the decisions made by the characters, a wealth of conflicts arise, providing endless inspiration for stories.”

Robert is also the founder of Rolldark Game Master Agency. He explains,

"There are many Dungeon Masters, each offering something unique to players. What we've done at Rolldark is curating a group of DMs that offer more, not just in terms of storytelling but also in terms of experience and depth. I believe that being a DM is a skill and a talent that should be celebrated as a profession."
